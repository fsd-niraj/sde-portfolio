My Journey
From Aspiring Developer to Seasoned Professional

My journey into the world of technology began with a Bachelor's in Computer Applications (BCA), where I laid the foundation for my passion for software development. Early on, I had the privilege of joining an ed-tech startup as a software developer, where I spent two transformative years honing my skills and embracing the challenges of innovation. It was more than just a job; it was an invaluable learning experience that shaped my approach to problem-solving and ignited my love for creating impactful solutions.

Eager for growth, I pursued further education in Enterprise Software Development and Web Development in Toronto, Canada. Immersed in a rich learning environment, I delved into networking, cloud computing, and cybersecurity, broadening my expertise and perspective.

Today, fueled by a passion for technology and a hunger for innovation, I stand at the threshold of endless possibilities. My journey is just beginning, and I'm excited to continue shaping the future through meaningful contributions in the ever-evolving tech landscape.
